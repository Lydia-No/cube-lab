# cube-lab

Workspace for experiments exploring symbolic systems on hypercube
structures.

cube-lab acts as a sandbox for developing new tools, simulations, and
visualizations related to symbolic cube exploration.

---

## Focus

Research topics explored here include:

- symbolic trajectories
- hypercube dynamics
- attractor detection
- symbolic sequence evolution
- automated rule exploration

---

## Motivation

Hypercubes appear naturally in many computational systems because they
represent combinations of binary features.

Exploring symbolic dynamics on these structures may reveal unexpected
patterns in conceptual state spaces.
